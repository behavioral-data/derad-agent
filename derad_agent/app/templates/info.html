About this Bot

This bot was created by University of Washington researchers as part of a study to investigate how AI bots affect online political conversations. Our bot generates responses by retrieving and synthesizing data from Community Notes, X's crowd-sourced fact-checking platform. We source notes from X's publicly available repository of all Community Notes data, which includes notes that have not yet been published publicly on the X platform. Responses may contain inaccurate information; please consult linked sources to verify any claims.
